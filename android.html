How to install PVZ 2C

Home About Contact

Look up 360 app china and click the first link

Click the blue button to install

After installing go to downloads and launch the APK

Open the app and download PVZ 2C

Once opened go to the search bar and type in 植物大战僵尸 and click the blue download button at the bottom.

In order to play, you need to register an account

Click the create account button in the bottom right corner and then create an account with your email and password of your choosing.
