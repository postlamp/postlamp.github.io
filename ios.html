How to install PVZ 2C

Home About Contact

Sign out of your main Apple account and create a Chinese one

Install the game

After changing your region to China, you can then just search "PVZ 2"

in the search bar and install the game. It can take a while to finish installing so be patient

In order to play, you need to register an account

Click the create account button in the bottom right corner and then create an account with your email and password of your choosing.

Congrats you did it!
